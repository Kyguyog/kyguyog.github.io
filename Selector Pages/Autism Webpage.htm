Autism

What is Autism?

Autism, or Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), is a complex neurodevelopmental disorder characterized by challenges with social interaction, communication, and repetitive behaviors. It affects individuals differently and to varying degrees.

Symptoms

  • Difficulty with communication and social interactions
  • Repetitive behaviors or restricted interests
  • Challenges in understanding social cues
  • Varied sensory sensitivities

Causes

The exact causes of autism are not fully understood, but research suggests that a combination of genetic and environmental factors may contribute to the development of the disorder.

Support and Resources

There are many resources available for people with autism, including:

  • Support groups
  • Therapies (e.g., speech, occupational)
  • Educational resources
  • Online communities
